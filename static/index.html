Workflow

Crisis resolved in four steps

From first report to resource dispatch — CrisisNexus compresses hours of coordination into minutes.

01
📸
Incident Reported
Any citizen taps SOS or submits a voice/photo report in their language — even offline via mesh.
02
🧠
Gemini Triage <3s
Vision AI analyzes the scene, assigns P1–P3, detects hazards and estimates casualties instantly.
03
⚡
C Engine Dispatches
Our native C geospatial engine computes nearest units via haversine, dispatches with real ETA.
04
📊
Command Oversight
Commanders monitor every unit and volunteer live. Vertex AI predicts shortages 30–60 min ahead.
